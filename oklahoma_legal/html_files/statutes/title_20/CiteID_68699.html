  Title 20. Courts
    Chapter 2 - Court of Criminal Appeals
        Section 40 - Exclusive Appellate Jurisdiction
 Cite as: O.S. §, __ __

The Criminal Court of Appeals shall have exclusive appellate jurisdiction, coextensive with the limits of the state, in all criminal cases appealed from the district, superior and county courts, and such other courts of record as may be established by law.

Historical Data

R.L. 1910, § 1769; [C.S. 1921, § 3047]; [St. 1931, § 3807].
