    Chapter 8 - Administrative Procedures Act
        Section 250.1 - Composition of Administrative Procedures Act
 Cite as: 75 O.S. § 250.1 (OSCN 2025)

A. The Administrative Procedures Act shall be composed of two articles. Sections 250, 250.1, 250.3, 250.4, 250.5, and 250.8 of this title are applicable to both Articles I and II. Article I relating to agency filing and publication requirements for rules shall consist of Sections 250.2, 250.4, 250.4a, 250.6, 250.7, and 250.9 through 308.3 of this title. Article II relating to agency notice and hearing requirements for individual proceedings shall consist of Sections 308a through 323 of this title.

B. Except as otherwise specifically provided in Section 250.4 of this title, all agencies shall comply with the provisions of Article I and Article II of the Administrative Procedures Act.
