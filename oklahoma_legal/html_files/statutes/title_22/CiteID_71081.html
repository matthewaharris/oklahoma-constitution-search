Title 22. Criminal Procedure

Oklahoma Statutes Citationized
  Title 22. Criminal Procedure
    Chapter 26 - Oklahoma Racketeer-Influenced and Corrupt Organizations Act
        Section 1401 - Short Title
 Cite as: 22 O.S. § 1401 (OSCN 2025)

Sections 1401 through 1419 of this title shall be known and may be cited as the "Oklahoma Racketeer-Influenced and Corrupt Organizations Act".

Historical Data

Laws 1988, SB 127, c. 131, § 1, eff. November 1, 1988; Amended by Laws 2010, HB 2983, c. 456, § 4, eff. November 1, 2010 (superseded document available).
