NOTICE CONCERNING TITLE 85 of the OKLAHOMA STATUTES

 

In 2011, the Oklahoma Legislature substantially revised the statutes relating to workers compensation. The Legislature replaced the Workers Compensation Act (the Act) with the new Worlers Compensation Code (the Code), both by adding new provisions and by repealing and renumbering statutory provisions found in the previous Act.

For a crosswalk from the new Workers Compensation Code to the statutory provisions in the previous Act, click here.  A printable version of the crosswalk table is available here.

For a disposition table that shows where provisions of the previous Act can be found in the new Workers Compensation Code, click here.  A printable version of the disposition table is available here.

NOTE:  These tables and documents are intended as a research tool and are for informational purposes only.  They are subject to change without notice.
