Title 15. Contracts

Oklahoma Statutes Citationized
  Title 15. Contracts
    Chapter 3 - Contract Interpretation
        Section 154 - Language of Contract Governs
 Cite as: O.S. §, __ __

The language of a contract is to govern its interpretation, if the language is clear and explicit, and does not involve an absurdity.

Historical Data

R.L. 1910, § 948.
