Title 15. Contracts

Oklahoma Statutes Citationized
  Title 15. Contracts
    Chapter 3 - Contract Interpretation
        Section 152 - Interpreted to Give Effect to Mutual Intention of Parties
 Cite as: O.S. §, __ __

A contract must be so interpreted as to give effect to the mutual intention of the parties, as it existed at the time of contracting, so far as the same is ascertainable and lawful.

Historical Data

R.L. 1910, § 946.
