Title 15. Contracts

Oklahoma Statutes Citationized
  Title 15. Contracts
    Chapter 3 - Contract Interpretation
        Section 157 - Whole of Contract Taken Together
 Cite as: O.S. §, __ __

The whole of a contract is to be taken together, so as to give effect to every part, if reasonably practicable, each clause helping to interpret the others.

Historical Data

R.L. 1910, § 951.
