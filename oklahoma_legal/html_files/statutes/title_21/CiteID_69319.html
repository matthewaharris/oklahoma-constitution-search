  Title 21. Crimes and Punishments
    Chapter 24 - Homicide
      Manslaughter
        Section 716 - Second Degree Manslaughter

This Statute Will Go Into Effect
Effective On: 01/01/2026
See Historical Data for Current Version
 Cite as: 21 O.S. § 716 (OSCN 2025), Manslaughter

Every killing of one human being by the act, procurement or culpable negligence of another, which, under the provisions of this chapter, is not murder, nor manslaughter in the first degree, nor excusable nor justifiable homicide, is manslaughter in the second degree, a Class B5 felony offense.

Historical Data

R.L. 1910, § 2325; Amended by Laws 2025, HB 2104, c. 486, § 156, eff. January 1, 2026 (superseded document available).
