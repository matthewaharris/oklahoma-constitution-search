  Title 21. Crimes and Punishments
    Chapter 68 - Larceny
        Section 1701 - Definition of Larceny
 Cite as: O.S. §, __ __

Larceny is the taking of personal property accomplished by fraud or stealth, and with intent to deprive another thereof.

Historical Data

R.L. 1910, § 2652.
