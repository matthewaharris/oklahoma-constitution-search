  Title 12. Civil Procedure
    Chapter 40 - Oklahoma Evidence Code
        Article Article VII - Expert and Opinion Testimony
        Section 2702 - Testimony by Experts
 Cite as: 12 O.S. § 2702 (OSCN 2025)

If scientific, technical, or other specialized knowledge will assist the trier of fact to understand the evidence or to determine a fact in issue, a witness qualified as an expert by knowledge, skill, experience, training, or education may testify in the form of an opinion or otherwise, if the proponent demonstrates to the court that it is more likely than not that:

1. The experts scientific, technical, or other specialized knowledge will help the trier of fact to understand the evidence or to determine a fact in issue;

2. The testimony is based upon sufficient facts or data;

3. The testimony is the product of reliable principles and methods; and

4. The experts opinion reflects a reliable application of the principles and methods to the facts of the case.
