Title 12. Civil Procedure

Oklahoma Statutes Citationized
  Title 12. Civil Procedure
    Chapter 5 - Venue
        Section 134 - Domestic Corporations
 Cite as: 12 O.S. § 134 (OSCN 2025)

An action, other than one described in Sections 131 through 133 of this title, against a limited liability company or corporation created by the laws of this state may be brought in the county in which it is situated or has its principal office or place of business, in which any of the members, managers or principal officers thereof may reside or be summoned, where the cause of action or some part thereof arose, or where a codefendant of such limited liability company or corporation created by the laws of this state may properly be sued.

Historical Data

R.L. 1910, § 4674; Amended by Laws 1913, HB 608, c. 83, p. 133, § 1; Amended by Laws 1970, HB 1641, c. 190, § 2, eff. January 1, 1971; Amended by Laws 2023, SB 617, c. 309, § 1, eff. November 1, 2023 (superseded document available).
